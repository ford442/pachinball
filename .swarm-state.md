## Adventure Track-Switch Teardown + Rebuild ‚Äî 2026-05-29 (Iteration 3)

### Objective
Wire the missing orchestration so that a portal jump from track A ‚Üí track B actually tears down old geometry/physics, builds the new track, fires the track-start cinematic, and resets the goal UI.

### Approach
Chose to put the heavy lifting in `game-slot-adventure.ts` and keep `game-systems-init.ts` thin:
1. `AdventureProgressionSupervisor.onPortalEntered()` reordered so `reset()` happens **before** `advanceToNextTrack()`. This makes it safe for the `onTrackAdvanced` callback to call `startTrack()` without having it overwritten.
2. `AdventureMode.switchToTrack(newZone)` added ‚Äî tears down old track via extracted `clearTrack()`, builds new track, updates camera preset, and emits `ZONE_ENTER`. Does NOT touch `followCamera` or `adventureActive` state.
3. `GameSlotAdventure.switchToTrack(trackId)` added ‚Äî validates track ID, calls `adventureMode.switchToTrack()`, then drives `adventureCinematicTriggers.onTrackStart()`, `adventureUIStateManager.reset()`, `adventureGoalTracker.initializeTrack()`, `adventureProgressionSupervisor.startTrack()`, and display updates.
4. `GameSystemsInitializer` wired `onTrackAdvanced` in `AdventureProgressionSupervisor` construction to delegate to `slotAdventure.switchToTrack()`.
5. Removed the redundant `setCurrentTrack` / `initializeTrack` / `startTrack` / `setTrackInfo` block from the `PORTAL_ENTERED` handler in `GameSystemsInitializer` since the callback now handles it.

## Feedback Layer (Combo / Ball-Save / Bonus Tally) ‚Äî 2026-06-05

### Objective
Deliver three interlocking feedback systems routed exclusively through the typed EventBus and DisplaySystem to convert the physics demo feel into a cohesive pinball experience.

### Pillar 1 ‚Äî Combo / Multiplier Escalation ‚úÖ
**New pure logic:** `src/game-elements/combo-multiplier-system.ts`
- Rolling combo window: consecutive scoring hits refresh a 2.5 s timer.
- Every 2 hits advance the multiplier by +1√ó (1√ó ‚Üí 2√ó ‚Üí 3√ó ‚Ä¶, capped at 5√ó).
- Resets on idle expiry, drain, or game reset.

**Wiring:**
- `GamePhysicsController` calls `comboMultiplierSystem.registerHit()` on every scoring obstacle hit (bumper, target, spinner, trap, launcher, gate).
- `awardScore()` now multiplies `multiballMultiplier * comboMultiplier`.
- Emits `combo:multiplier:changed` on level-up.
- `Game` exposes `comboMultiplier` field; `GameHUD` passes total multiplier to `updateHUD()`.
- `DisplaySystem` subscribes and flashes `MULTIPLIER xN` on the backbox text overlay.

### Pillar 2 ‚Äî Ball-Save Grace Window ‚úÖ
**New pure logic:** `src/game-elements/ball-save-system.ts`
- 7-second grace window after ball launch.
- One-time save: early drain respawns at plunger without life loss or score reset.

**Wiring:**
- `BallManager` owns `ballSaveSystem`; `startBallSaveGraceWindow()` restarts the window.
- `game.ts` `onPlunger` callback emits `ball:launched` and calls `ballManager?.startBallSaveGraceWindow()`.
- `GamePhysicsController.handleBallLoss()` checks `ballManager?.ballSaveSystem.canSave()` before life-loss logic.
- On save: emits `ball:save:triggered` + `sound:play('ball-save')`, shows "BALL SAVED" HUD message.
- `DisplaySystem` subscribes and shows "BALL SAVED" on the backbox.

### Pillar 3 ‚Äî End-of-Ball Bonus Tally ‚úÖ
**New pure logic:** `src/game-elements/bonus-tally-system.ts`
- Accumulates per-ball bonuses: chain bonuses, gold-ball collects, and a peak-combo bonus (250 pts per peak hit).

**Wiring:**
- `GamePhysicsController` records chain bonuses and gold-ball collects into the bucket.
- Combo peak is captured on combo expiry and on drain.
- `sweepBonusTally()` emits `bonus:tally:start` (with breakdown), `effect:bloom`, and `effect:flash` for cabinet celebration; adds total to score; emits `bonus:tally:complete` after animation.
- `DisplaySystem` runs a frame-driven count-up animation on the backbox text overlay.
